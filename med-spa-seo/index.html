What's Costing You Rankings

Common SEO Mistakes Med Spas Make

Most med spas have an SEO foundation full of holes — and they don't even know it. Here are the issues we fix in the first 30 days of every engagement.

Ignoring Google Business Profile

Treating GBP as a one-time setup instead of an active marketing channel. No weekly posts, no Q&A, no fresh photos. This single mistake costs more rankings than anything else.

One Generic "Services" Page

Listing every treatment on one page. Google needs a dedicated, optimized page for each treatment to rank you for it. Botox, fillers, laser, CoolSculpting all need their own home.

Inconsistent NAP Across the Web

Wrong suite number on Yelp, old phone on Yellow Pages, misspelled name on Healthgrades. Every inconsistency tells Google "we're not sure this is the same business" — and you lose ranking signal.

No Review Strategy

Hoping happy patients leave a Google review. Most won't unless you ask — and ask immediately. Review velocity (new reviews per month) is one of the top map pack ranking factors.

No Schema Markup

Missing MedicalProcedure, LocalBusiness, and FAQ schema. Schema is how you tell Google exactly what your page is about — and it powers rich results that take up more SERP real estate.

Slow, Bloated Website

Core Web Vitals are a confirmed ranking factor. Sites that take 5+ seconds to load on mobile get demoted. We fix page speed, image bloat, and render-blocking scripts.

Targeting "Med Spa" Only

Ignoring the long tail. "Botox for forehead lines [city]" converts 10x higher than "med spa" — and is far easier to rank. Long-tail keywords are how new clinics break into competitive markets.

No YMYL Trust Signals

Medical content is held to higher Google standards. No medical reviewer bio, no credentials, no citations — and Google buries your pages no matter how well-written they are.
