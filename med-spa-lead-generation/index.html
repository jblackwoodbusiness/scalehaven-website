What's Holding You Back

Common Lead Generation Challenges

If any of these sound familiar, you're not alone. These are the problems we solve for every client we work with.

Feast-or-Famine Patient Flow

Busy one month, empty the next. Without a predictable pipeline, revenue swings wildly and staffing decisions become guesswork. A real lead system creates consistency.

Leads That Ghost

60-70% of leads never respond after initial contact because follow-up is too slow or nonexistent. By the time someone calls back, the lead has moved on.

Price Shoppers & Tire-Kickers

Leads from generic ads attract bargain hunters, not ideal patients. Without qualifying questions and intent-based targeting, you waste time on people who were never going to book.

Staff Overwhelmed with Follow-Up

Your front desk is already maxed out with check-ins, scheduling, and patient care. Calling leads falls to the bottom of the priority list — and conversion rates tank.

No Tracking or Attribution

Most practices have no idea which marketing channel actually drives booked patients. Without attribution, every budget decision is a guess.

Rising Ad Costs

Meta CPMs are up 30%+ year over year in competitive markets. Without constant optimization and creative testing, ad budgets burn fast with diminishing returns.

HIPAA Compliance Concerns

Fear of violating patient privacy regulations limits what many practices are willing to try in advertising. The result: they play it safe and miss out on proven channels.

Low Rebooking Rates

Getting a new patient once is hard — getting them back is where the real revenue lives. With rebooking rates at 40-50%, most clinics lose patients after the first visit.
