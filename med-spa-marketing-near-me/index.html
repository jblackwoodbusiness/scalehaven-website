Agency Selection Guide

What to Look for in a Med Spa Marketing Agency

Not all agencies are built the same. Before you sign with anyone — local or remote — measure them against these eight criteria.

Niche Experience

Do they specialize in med spas or are you one of 20 different industries? Ask for treatment-specific case studies.

Full-Funnel Approach

Ads alone don't book patients. Do they handle follow-up, automation, and booking? Or just hand you leads? A real lead generation system goes from click to consultation.

Performance Guarantee

Agencies that guarantee results have confidence in their system. If they won't put skin in the game, that's a red flag.

Transparent Reporting

You should know exactly how many leads came in, how many booked, cost per lead, cost per consultation, and ROI — weekly.

HIPAA Awareness

Medical marketing has compliance requirements general marketers don't understand. Your agency needs to know what you can and can't say in ads.

Speed of Follow-Up

Ask how they handle lead response time. If the answer isn't "within 60 seconds, automatically" — they're leaving money on the table.

Creative Strategy

They should create ad copy and creative for you, not just manage campaigns. The best-performing ads use real clinic content, not stock photos.

No Long-Term Lock-In

Results-driven agencies don't need 12-month contracts to keep clients. Month-to-month after an initial period signals confidence.
