Common Meta Ads Mistakes Med Spas Make

Most med spas waste $2,000-5,000/month on Meta Ads that don't work — not because the platform is broken, but because the execution is. Here are the mistakes we fix on day one.

Boosting Posts Instead of Real Campaigns

Boosted posts have no lead objective, no form, no optimization. It's burning money for likes. Real campaigns use the Leads objective with Instant Forms to drive actual bookings.

Targeting Too Broad

"Women 25-65 interested in beauty" is not targeting. Layered interest + behavior + lookalike audiences perform 3-5x better. We build custom audience stacks for every campaign.

No Follow-Up System

Getting leads is step one. Without instant automated follow-up, 70%+ of leads go cold within 24 hours. Speed to contact is everything.

Using Stock Photos Instead of Real Content

Authentic video and photos from your actual clinic outperform stock imagery by 2-4x in engagement and conversion. Patients want to see your results, not a model.

Not Testing Creative

Running the same ad for months is a guaranteed way to watch results die. Creative fatigue sets in after 7-14 days. We rotate 8-12 variations per campaign to keep performance high.

Ignoring Retargeting

Only running cold campaigns. Warm audiences — website visitors, video viewers, form abandoners — convert at 3-5x the rate of cold traffic. We build retargeting into every funnel.

Wrong Campaign Objective

Running traffic or engagement campaigns when the goal is leads. The algorithm optimizes for what you tell it — if you optimize for clicks, you get clickers, not patients.

No Tracking Setup

Without proper Pixel + CAPI (Conversions API), Meta can't optimize for the leads most likely to book. We set up full tracking infrastructure before a single ad runs.
