What's Burning Your Budget

Common Google Ads Mistakes Med Spas Make

Most clinics running Google Ads waste 40-60% of their budget on the wrong keywords, weak landing pages, and broken tracking. Here are the mistakes we fix immediately.

Broad Match With No Negatives

Running broad match keywords without an aggressive negative keyword list is the fastest way to burn money. We add 200+ negatives on day one before a click is bought.

Sending Traffic to Homepage

A Botox searcher landing on a general med spa homepage is a 90% bounce. Dedicated treatment landing pages convert 3-5x higher. This single fix usually halves cost per lead.

No Conversion Tracking

If Google's algorithm doesn't know which clicks become patients, it can't optimize. Most accounts we audit have tracking misfiring or tracking the wrong events entirely.

Ignoring Local Service Ads

LSAs sit above paid search and only charge per lead, not per click. Eligible med spas skipping LSA are leaving 20-40% cheaper leads on the table for competitors.

No Retargeting Setup

90%+ of first-time visitors don't book. Without Display + YouTube retargeting, all that paid traffic is gone forever. Retargeted visitors convert at 5-7x cold rates.

Letting Smart Bidding Run Blind

Google's automated bidding works — but only when fed clean conversion data and proper bid floors. Otherwise it spends your full budget chasing low-quality clicks.

Slow Mobile Landing Pages

70%+ of med spa search traffic is mobile. Pages slower than 3 seconds lose 40% of visitors before they see the booking form. Speed is conversion.

No Follow-Up on Phone Leads

Google Ads drives a lot of calls. Missed calls = missed patients. Without call tracking and instant callback systems, 30-50% of paid leads vanish.
